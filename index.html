What is Mika AI?

Mika AI is a powerful, all-in-one Artificial Intelligence software designed for Windows. Unlike other AI tools that require expensive subscriptions or heavy cloud usage, Mika AI runs locally on your computer.

It combines the power of Llama 3 (for chat) and Stable Diffusion (for art) to give you a premium experience for free. Whether you are a student, creator, or researcher, Mika AI is built to empower you.

Key Features

Everything you need in one software

Smart Chat (Llama 3)

Intelligent conversation, coding help, and creative writing. Completely offline.

AI Image Generation

Turn text into stunning images instantly using Stable Diffusion v1.5. Unlimited generations.

Video Generation (Beta)

Convert your static images into short motion videos (Image-to-Video).

Deep Research

Need real-time info? Switch to Research Mode to fetch live data from the web via DuckDuckGo.

Document Analysis

Upload PDF files and chat with them. Summarize or ask questions from your documents.

100% Offline & Private

Your data stays on your PC. No hidden servers, no tracking. Privacy first.

System Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  • Intel Core i5 (8th Gen) / AMD Ryzen 5
  • 8 GB RAM
  • 10 GB Free Space (SSD)

Recommended (Best Performance)

  • RAM: 16 GB or higher
  • GPU: NVIDIA Graphics Card (4GB VRAM)
  • * GPU is optional but recommended for faster video generation.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Mika AI processes 99% of tasks offline using your computer's hardware. We do not collect, store, or sell your chat history, generated images, or personal files. All generated content is saved locally in your 'generated_outputs' folder.
